Biography

Hey — I'm Ashen. I'm a web development intern currently working my way through the full stack of building real, live websites. One task at a time, one bug at a time.

I grew up in India and picked up HTML during a school holiday that was supposed to be a break. It wasn't. The moment I understood that a <button> and a <div onclick> are genuinely different things — not just stylistically but for real users — I was hooked.

Accessibility is what I care most about right now. The idea that the same HTML I write could either help or completely block someone using a screen reader sits with me. I'd rather get the fundamentals right than chase whatever framework is trending this month.

Outside code: hiking when the weather agrees, reading non-fiction, and convincing myself I'll eventually get better at chess.
